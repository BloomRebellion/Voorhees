Entry 01

The Jakob Sandwich

Inspired by Cheba Hut's White Widow, named after our firstborn.

Ingredients

  • 1 ciabatta roll
  • 1 boneless chicken breast, oven roasted
  • 2–3 slices bacon, cooked
  • 2 slices provolone cheese
  • Fresh tomato, sliced
  • Ranch dressing

Steps

  1. Toast the ciabatta roll.
  2. Layer the oven roasted chicken on the bottom half.
  3. Top with provolone and melt.
  4. Add bacon and fresh tomato slices.
  5. Finish with ranch and close the sandwich.

Someday, Jakob, there's a sandwich with your name on it. Literally.
